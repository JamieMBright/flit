2. Description of Service

Flit is a geography-based flight game where players fly a hand-drawn plane over a GPU-rendered globe, receive clues about target countries or regions, and attempt to land on the correct location. The game is available on iOS, Android, and Web.

The Service includes:

  • Single-player geography challenges across world and regional modes
  • Head-to-head asynchronous multiplayer challenges
  • Leaderboards and player statistics
  • Cosmetic customisation (planes, contrails, companions)
