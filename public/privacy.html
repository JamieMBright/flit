Privacy Policy
Last updated: 21 February 2026

Overview

Flit is a geography-based flight game. We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes what data we collect, how it is stored, how long we keep it, and what rights you have over it.

Short version: We collect the minimum data needed to make the game work. We do not sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.

Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the game and improve reliability.

Data Why Stored
Email address Account creation, login, and account recovery Supabase (encrypted)
Username Your visible pilot name in the game Supabase
Game statistics Scores, round times, games played, accuracy — so progress is saved across devices Supabase
Error telemetry Crash reports to help us fix bugs — contains error messages, platform (web/iOS/Android), and app version only Vercel (ephemeral)

What error telemetry does NOT include:

Error logs never contain your email, username, device identifier, IP address, or browser fingerprint. The platform field is a coarse label (web-browser, ios-device, android-device) — not a User-Agent string.

How Your Data Is Stored

Account data (email, username, game statistics) is stored in Supabase, a cloud PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure. Connections are TLS-encrypted. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt — we never see your plaintext password.

Error telemetry is received by a Vercel serverless function and held in an ephemeral in-memory buffer. Errors are periodically flushed to a private GitHub repository log file for triage, then discarded. Vercel functions are stateless — the buffer is cleared on every cold start. Error logs are not linked to any user account.

We do not operate our own database servers. All infrastructure is managed by reputable third-party providers (Supabase, Vercel, GitHub) under their own security programs.

Data Retention

Data type Retention
Account data (email, username, stats) Kept until you delete your account. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
Error telemetry Ephemeral. Purged from memory on every server restart. GitHub log entries are pruned regularly.
Local storage (web) Auth session token only. Cleared when you sign out.

Your Rights

You have the following rights over your data, exercisable directly from within the app:

  • Right to access — You can view all your game statistics and profile data from the Profile screen at any time.
  • Right to export — You can export all your data as a JSON file from the Profile screen. The export includes your username, email, and full game history.
  • Right to deletion — You can delete your account from the Profile screen. This permanently removes your email, username, and all associated game data from Supabase. Deletion is irreversible.
  • Right to rectification — You can update your username and display name from within the app.

To exercise any right not available in-app, or to request deletion of historical error log entries, contact us at jamiebright1@gmail.com.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies.

The only third-party services that process your data are our infrastructure providers (Supabase and Vercel), which act as data processors on our behalf under their own privacy policies and Terms of Service. They do not use your data for their own purposes.

No Analytics or Tracking

Flit does not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any other third-party analytics SDK. There are no tracking pixels, ad networks, or behavioural profiling tools in the app.

The only telemetry is the crash/error reporting described above, which contains no personally identifiable information.

Cookies & Local Storage

The Flit web app does not use cookies. On web, we use localStorage solely to persist your Supabase auth session token between page loads, so you do not have to log in every time you open the app. No other data is written to local storage.

Native iOS and Android apps use the platform's secure keychain/keystore for session persistence — no cookies are involved.

Children & COPPA

Flit is a geography education game suitable for all ages. However, creating an account requires an email address. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account without your consent, please contact us at jamiebright1@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes that affect how we handle your data, we will notify you by email at the address associated with your account.

Continued use of Flit after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
